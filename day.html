On the Day

The Ceremony

1pm for 1:30 pm

If we are lucky with the weather the ceremony will be held at Cellardyke Harbour (1 Shore Street, KY10 3BD). If it's too chilly or rainy we will have the ceremony in Cellardyke Town Hall. (1 John Street, KY10 3BA)

Drinks Reception

After the ceremony we will enjoy some bubbles at the harbour or town hall. Photos will also be taken at this time so get your smiles at the ready.

The Treasure Hunt

After the drinks reception we will make our way to Anstruther town hall. This is a short (10 min) walk so please bring sensible shoes, and if the weather gods aren't with us, a warm jacket. Don't forget to grab your treasure map before you leave and spot the clues on the way (prizes to be won).

The Cup and Saucer Tea

4pm

Dinner will be an old school High Tea, served from 4pm. It will include the local delicacy of Fish and Chips followed by minature cakes from another local favourite, Fisher and Donaldsons bakers. Speeches will follow.

Cutting the Cake

After dinner we hope you can enjoy a visit to the whisky or gin bar and join us for the cutting of the cake.

Evening Do

7:30pm to 1:00am

Join us in Anstruther Town Hall for a bit of ceilidh dancing....don't worry we will have all the classic wedding hits too! Last orders at midnight.

18 School Grn, Anstruther KY10 3DF

The Bar and Buffet

We will provide a free bar for the evening with a range of wine, beer, cider and spirits to cater for most tastes. If you would like anything more obscure please feel free to bring it along. There is no corkage or anything to worry about. An evening buffet will also be provided to keep everyone's energy up after all the boogeying.

Getting home

1:00am

We will provide a bus to drop off at the nearby villages at 1am. Please see the Where to Stay page for details
